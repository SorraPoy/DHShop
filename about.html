About DHShop

A home for building and playing your curated game collection,

A curated selection of games

From exceptional AAAs, unique indies to the best of classic gaming. Every game is here because we chose it for you.

Feeding your inner collector

Offering games with as many goodies as possible is the DHShop way - even if it means exploring our long forgotten basements and attics.

Every game deserves to shine

As much attention we're giving to picking great games, the same goes for highlighting them on DHShop - treating each release as a celebration.
